Morning Prayer

Welcome to daily-prayer.appspot.com, home of the open source website for your daily prayers. These prayers are taken from the Book of Common Prayer of the Episcopal Church, which includes prayers collected through the centuries. Prayers will update daily, and should work well on your mobile device so you can pray wherever you are.
